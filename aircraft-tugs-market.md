<p><strong>The Aircraft Tugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Towbars</li><li>Towbarless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft tugs market is primarily segmented into two types: conventional towbars and towbarless systems. Conventional towbars utilize a connecting device that attaches to the aircraft's nose gear, allowing for traditional towing by ground vehicles. In contrast, towbarless tugs employ a unique design that lifts the aircraft from its body, providing greater maneuverability and reducing the risk of damage to the aircraft. Both types cater to different operational needs, enhancing ground handling efficiency in various aviation environments.</p></p>
