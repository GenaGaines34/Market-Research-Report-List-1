<p><p>The Handheld Vision Screeners market is divided into two main types: monocular and binocular measurement. Monocular measurement devices assess vision in one eye at a time, providing quick screenings and simplicity, making them user-friendly for various settings. In contrast, binocular measurement devices evaluate both eyes simultaneously, offering a more comprehensive assessment of binocular vision and coordination. Each type serves distinct needs in vision screening, catering to different environments such as clinics, schools, and telemedicine applications.</p></p>
