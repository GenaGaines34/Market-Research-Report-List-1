<p><ul><li>Solvent</li><li>Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polyaspartic Coatings market is primarily divided into solvent-based and powder-based types. Solvent-based polyaspartic coatings are formulated with organic solvents, offering excellent adhesion and flexibility, making them suitable for various applications, including industrial and commercial flooring. In contrast, powder-based polyaspartic coatings are free of solvents, resulting in lower volatile organic compound emissions. They provide durable, environmentally friendly options for finishes in automotive, architectural, and protective coatings, appealing to sustainability-conscious consumers while ensuring high-performance characteristics.</p></p>
