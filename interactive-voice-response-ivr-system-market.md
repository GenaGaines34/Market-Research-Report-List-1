<p><p>The Interactive Voice Response (IVR) system market is categorized into two main types: speech-based and touch-tone based. Speech-based IVR allows users to interact using natural language, enabling more intuitive and flexible communication. This technology leverages voice recognition to understand and process user requests. In contrast, touch-tone based IVR requires users to respond by pressing numeric keys on a phone keypad, providing a straightforward interaction method. Both systems enhance customer service efficiency, catering to different user preferences and technological capabilities.</p></p>
