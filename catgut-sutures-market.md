The market for catgut sutures primarily consists of two types: chromic catgut sutures and plain catgut sutures. Chromic catgut is treated with chromium salts, which extend its absorption time, making it suitable for longer healing processes. In contrast, plain catgut sutures are absorbed quickly, offering a faster healing option. Both types cater to different surgical requirements, influencing their demand in the healthcare market.</p></p>
