<p><ul><li>Single Piston Caliper</li><li>Multi-Piston Caliper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brake calipers market comprises two primary types: single piston calipers and multi-piston calipers. Single piston calipers utilize one piston to apply pressure on the brake pad, making them simpler and more cost-effective, often found in compact and economical vehicles. In contrast, multi-piston calipers feature multiple pistons that provide enhanced braking force and improved heat dissipation, making them suitable for high-performance and luxury vehicles. Each type caters to specific vehicle performance needs and market segments.</p></p>
