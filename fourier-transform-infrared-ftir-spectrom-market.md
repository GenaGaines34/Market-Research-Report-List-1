<p><p>The Fourier Transform Infrared (FTIR) spectrom market is segmented into three key types: Far-infrared FTIR, Mid-infrared FTIR, and Near-infrared FTIR. Far-infrared FTIR primarily analyzes low-energy molecular vibrations, useful for studying larger biomolecules. Mid-infrared FTIR focuses on fundamental molecular vibrations, providing detailed information on chemical bonds and functional groups, widely applied in organic chemistry and materials science. Near-infrared FTIR, operating at higher energy levels, is effective for quick, non-destructive analysis, commonly used in agricultural and pharmaceutical industries.</p></p>
