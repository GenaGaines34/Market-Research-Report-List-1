<p><ul><li>Halogen Light Source</li><li>LED Light Source</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental light curing machine market is primarily segmented into two types: halogen light sources and LED light sources. Halogen light sources have been traditional in dental practices, providing strong, broad-spectrum light for curing dental materials. However, they generate heat and require longer curing times. LED light sources, on the other hand, are more energy-efficient, produce minimal heat, and offer faster curing times with specific wavelengths tailored for various dental composites. This shift towards LED technology is enhancing efficiency and comfort in dental procedures.</p></p>
