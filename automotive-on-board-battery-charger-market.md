<p><p>The automotive on-board battery charger market comprises two main types: isolated and non-isolated chargers. Isolated chargers provide electrical separation between the input and output, enhancing safety and reducing noise, which is vital for sensitive electronic components. Non-isolated chargers, on the other hand, offer a more compact design with a direct connection, leading to higher efficiency and lower costs. Both types serve to efficiently charge electric and hybrid vehicle batteries, supporting the growing demand for sustainable automotive solutions.</p></p>
