<p><p>Honing steel is essential for maintaining and refining the edge of cutting tools. It comes in three main types: ceramic, diamond, and steel. Ceramic honing steel is known for its hardness and ability to restore edge precision without removing significant material. Diamond honing steel, embedded with diamond particles, offers superior sharpening efficiency and durability. Steel honing steel is the traditional choice, providing effective maintenance for many knives but may require more frequent use to achieve optimal results.</p></p>
