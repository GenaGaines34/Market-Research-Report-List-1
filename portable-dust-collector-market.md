<p><ul><li>Bag</li><li>Cartridge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable dust collector market primarily includes two types: bag and cartridge collectors. Bag collectors utilize large fabric bags to capture dust particles, making them effective for heavy dust loads and larger particles. They often require periodic bag changes. Cartridge collectors, on the other hand, use cylindrical filters for finer particles and can maintain higher efficiency with smaller footprints. Both types offer mobility and versatility, catering to various industrial applications and enhancing air quality in workplaces.</p></p>
