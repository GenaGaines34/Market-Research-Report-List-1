<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic</li><li>Electromagnetic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive reversing radar market primarily includes two types: ultrasonic and electromagnetic systems. Ultrasonic sensors use sound waves to detect obstacles by measuring the time it takes for the waves to bounce back, making them effective for close-range detection. In contrast, electromagnetic systems utilize magnetic fields to identify objects behind the vehicle, offering broader coverage. Both technologies enhance safety during reversing by alerting drivers to potential hazards, thereby reducing accidents and improving overall driving experience.</p></p>
