<p><p>Signage lighting is categorized into three main types: front glowing, side glowing, and back glowing. Front glowing signage illuminates directly from the front, enhancing visibility and readability. Side glowing signage uses lateral lighting to create a soft glow along the edges, providing a subtle yet attractive effect. Back glowing signage features illumination behind the sign, producing a halo effect that adds depth and prominence. Each type serves unique aesthetic and functional purposes, catering to various advertising needs.</p></p>
