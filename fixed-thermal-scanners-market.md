<p><strong>The Fixed Thermal Scanners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR)</li><li>Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR)</li><li>Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fixed thermal scanners market is categorized into three main types: Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR), Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR), and Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR). SWIR scanners excel in detecting moisture content and providing detailed imaging in low-light conditions. MWIR scanners are effective for thermal imaging applications, particularly in industrial and military settings, due to their high sensitivity to temperature variations. LWIR scanners are widely used for surveillance and monitoring, as they can detect heat signatures in complete darkness, enhancing security and safety applications.</p></p>
