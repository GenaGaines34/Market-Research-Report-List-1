<p><p>The harbor tugboat market is categorized mainly into deep-sea and river tugboats. Deep-sea tugboats are designed for oceanic operations, assisting large vessels in navigating through challenging offshore conditions and port maneuvers. They often feature powerful engines and robust hulls. Conversely, river tugboats are built for inland waterways, characterized by their shallow drafts and maneuverability, enabling them to operate effectively in narrow channels and manage smaller vessels or barges in rivers and estuaries. Both types play crucial roles in maritime logistics.</p></p>
