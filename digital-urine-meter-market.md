<p><p>The Digital Urine Meter market is categorized into two main types: Single Function and Multifunction devices. Single Function meters are designed to perform a specific task, typically measuring urine output or specific gravity, providing straightforward data for clinical use. In contrast, Multifunction meters combine multiple features, allowing users to assess various parameters such as pH, glucose levels, and ketones simultaneously. This versatility in Multifunction devices caters to diverse clinical needs, enhancing efficiency and patient monitoring capabilities in healthcare settings.</p></p>
