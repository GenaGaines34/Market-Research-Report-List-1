<p><strong>The Pumped Hydro Storage Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed-loop System</li><li>Open-loop System</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pumped hydro storage consists of two primary market types: closed-loop and open-loop systems. Closed-loop systems use two reservoirs at different elevations, enabling water transfer for energy storage without external water sources, minimizing environmental impact. Open-loop systems rely on natural water bodies, sourcing water directly from rivers or lakes, which can affect local ecosystems. Additionally, other market segments include small-scale pumped storage, focusing on localized energy management and hybrid systems that integrate with renewable sources, enhancing grid reliability and efficiency.</p></p>
