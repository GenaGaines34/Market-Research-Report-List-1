<p><strong>The Dental Healing Cap Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight Healing Cap</li><li>Angled Healing Cap</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental healing cap market comprises two primary types: straight healing caps and angled healing caps. Straight healing caps are designed for a direct vertical orientation, promoting healing in straightforward implant placements. In contrast, angled healing caps facilitate the healing process when implants are positioned at an angle, providing better aesthetics and functional alignment for subsequent restorations. Both types serve to protect the implant site, promote tissue growth, and enhance the overall success of dental implants.</p></p>
