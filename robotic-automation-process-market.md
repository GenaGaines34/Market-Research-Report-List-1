<p><p>The Robotic Process Automation (RPA) market is primarily divided into two types: rule-based and knowledge-based automation. Rule-based automation follows predefined, repetitious tasks with structured data, making it ideal for straightforward processes like data entry. In contrast, knowledge-based automation utilizes advanced technologies like artificial intelligence and machine learning to handle unstructured data and complex decision-making. This enables it to adapt and learn from experiences, making it suitable for processes that require more cognitive capabilities and nuanced understanding. </p></p>
