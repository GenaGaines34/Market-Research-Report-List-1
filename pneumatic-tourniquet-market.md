<p><strong>The Pneumatic Tourniquet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Bladder Tourniquet</li><li>Dual Bladder Tourniquet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic tourniquet market comprises two main types: single bladder and dual bladder tourniquets. Single bladder tourniquets feature one inflatable compartment that exerts pressure, ideal for controlled occlusion in surgical procedures. In contrast, dual bladder tourniquets have two separate inflatable compartments, allowing for more precise pressure adjustment and enhanced safety by distributing pressure evenly. These innovations improve patient comfort and reduce the risk of complications, catering to varied medical needs in surgical and trauma settings.</p></p>
