<p><p>Ready Mixed Concrete (RMX) is a concrete type manufactured in a factory or batching plant and delivered to construction sites. The market is categorized into three types: Transit Mixed Concrete, mixed during transit in truck mixers; Shrink Mixed Concrete, partially mixed at the plant and completed during delivery; and Central Mixed Concrete, fully mixed at the plant and transported in a truck. Each type offers distinct advantages in quality control, convenience, and efficiency for various construction applications.</p></p>
