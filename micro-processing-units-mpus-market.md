<p><p>Micro Processing Units (MPUs) are categorized into two primary market types: Read-Only MPUs and Read/Write MPUs. Read-Only MPUs are designed to execute pre-stored instructions, primarily in applications requiring fixed functionalities, making them ideal for embedded systems. In contrast, Read/Write MPUs allow the modification of instructions and data, enabling greater flexibility in processing tasks and applications. This adaptability makes Read/Write MPUs suitable for a wide range of computing environments, including personal computers and advanced embedded systems.</p></p>
