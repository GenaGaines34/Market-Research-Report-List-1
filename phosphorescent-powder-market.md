<p><ul><li>Short Persistence Phosphorescent Pigment</li><li>Long Persistence Phosphorescent Pigment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The phosphorescent powder market is divided into two main types: short persistence phosphorescent pigments and long persistence phosphorescent pigments. Short persistence pigments emit light for a brief period after being exposed to a light source, making them suitable for applications requiring quick visibility, such as emergency signs. In contrast, long persistence pigments glow for extended durations, often lasting hours, making them ideal for use in decorative items and safety equipment, where prolonged luminescence is essential for visibility in darkness.</p></p>
