<p><p>The Japonica rice market is primarily categorized into two types: translucent rice and opaque rice. Translucent rice is characterized by its clear, shiny grains that exhibit a polished appearance, often preferred for its visual appeal and cooking quality. Opaque rice, on the other hand, is less polished, resulting in a milky-white appearance and often possessing a firmer texture when cooked. Both types are popular in various culinary applications, with translucent rice commonly used in sushi and dishes requiring a delicate presentation, while opaque rice is favored for heartier dishes.</p></p>
