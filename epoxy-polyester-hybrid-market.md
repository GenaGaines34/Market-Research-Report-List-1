<p><p>The Epoxy-Polyester Hybrid market primarily includes two types: powder and film. Powder coatings, known for their durability and resistance, are often applied electrostatically and cure through heat, making them ideal for protective and decorative finishes. Film coatings, on the other hand, provide a smooth and flexible surface and are applied through processes like spraying or rolling, offering versatility for various applications. Both types harness the benefits of epoxy and polyester, making them suitable for diverse industries, including automotive, electronics, and architecture.</p></p>
