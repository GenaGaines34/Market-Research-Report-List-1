<p><strong>The Sterilization Cabinets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Heat Sterilization Cabinet</li><li>Damp and Heat Sterilization Cabinet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sterilization cabinets play a crucial role in healthcare and laboratory settings by ensuring the safety and effectiveness of instruments and materials. The dry heat sterilization cabinet utilizes high temperatures to kill microorganisms through the conduction of heat, making it suitable for items that cannot tolerate moisture. In contrast, the damp and heat sterilization cabinet employs steam under pressure to achieve sterilization, effectively penetrating materials and killing bacteria. Both types are essential for maintaining hygiene and preventing contamination across various industries.</p></p>
