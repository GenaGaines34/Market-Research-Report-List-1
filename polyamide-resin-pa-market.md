<p><strong>The Polyamide Resin (PA) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reactive Type</li><li>Nonreactive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyamide resin (PA) is categorized into two main types: reactive and non-reactive. Reactive polyamide resins contain functional groups that enable chemical cross-linking during curing, enhancing mechanical properties and heat resistance, making them suitable for coatings and adhesives. Non-reactive polyamide resins, on the other hand, do not undergo such cross-linking and are typically used in applications requiring flexibility and resistance to chemicals, such as in textiles and plastics. Together, these types cater to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
