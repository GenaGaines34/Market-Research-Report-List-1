<p><strong>The Graphite Insulation Felts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rayon Based</li><li>PAN Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Graphite Insulation Felts market is primarily categorized into two types: Rayon-Based and PAN-Based felts. Rayon-based felts are produced from regenerated cellulose fibers, offering good thermal insulation properties and flexibility, making them suitable for various industrial applications. In contrast, PAN-based felts are derived from polyacrylonitrile fibers, providing superior thermal stability and mechanical strength, ideal for high-temperature environments. Both types serve critical roles in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and energy, ensuring effective insulation and thermal management solutions.</p></p>
