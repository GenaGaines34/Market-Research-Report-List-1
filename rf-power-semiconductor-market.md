<p><p>The RF power semiconductor market encompasses various components essential for radio frequency applications. RF power amplifiers increase signal strength for transmission, while RF passives, such as resistors and capacitors, manage signal flow without amplification. RF duplexers allow simultaneous transmission and reception on the same frequency, optimizing communications. RF switches enable routing of signals among various paths, enhancing flexibility in circuits. Other RF devices may include mixers, oscillators, and detectors, supporting a wide range of RF functionalities in telecommunications and electronics.</p></p>
