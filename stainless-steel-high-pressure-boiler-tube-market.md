<p><p>The stainless steel high-pressure boiler tube market is segmented into three primary types: ferritic, austenitic, and martensitic stainless steels, along with a category for other materials. Ferritic stainless steels offer excellent resistance to oxidation and are cost-effective, while austenitic stainless steels provide superior corrosion resistance and toughness at high temperatures. Martensitic stainless steels are known for their strength and hardness, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. Other materials may include duplex stainless steels and specialty alloys, catering to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
