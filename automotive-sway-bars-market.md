<p><p>The **Automotive Sway Bars Market** includes **Active** and **Physical** types. **Active sway bars** use electronic systems to adjust stiffness in real-time, enhancing vehicle stability and handling based on driving conditions. They are common in high-performance and luxury vehicles. **Physical sway bars** are fixed mechanical components that reduce body roll during cornering, improving safety and comfort. They are widely used in standard vehicles due to their cost-effectiveness and simplicity. Both types aim to optimize vehicle dynamics but differ in technology and application.</p></p>
