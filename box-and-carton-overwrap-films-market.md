<p><strong>The Box and Carton Overwrap Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shrink Films</li><li>Stretch Films</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Box and carton overwrap films are packaging materials used to protect and bundle products. Shrink films are heat-sensitive materials that shrink tightly around items when heated, providing a secure and tamper-proof seal; they are ideal for packaging irregular shapes. Stretch films, on the other hand, are elastic materials that stretch around products, offering a strong hold without heat application, making them suitable for stabilizing palletized loads during transportation. Both serve to enhance product protection and presentation.</p></p>
