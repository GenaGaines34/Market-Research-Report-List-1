<p><p>The Cutaneous Lupus Erythematosus (CLE) treatment market includes **topical** and **systemic** treatments. **Topical treatments** involve creams, gels, or ointments applied directly to the skin to reduce inflammation and symptoms. Common options include corticosteroids and calcineurin inhibitors. **Systemic treatments** are oral or injectable medications that work throughout the body to manage severe or widespread CLE. These include antimalarials, immunosuppressants, and biologics. Both approaches aim to control symptoms, prevent flares, and minimize skin damage, with treatment choice depending on disease severity and patient response.</p></p>
