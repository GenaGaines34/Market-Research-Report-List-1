<p><p>The Intermediate Bulk Container (IBC) market consists of two main types: rigid containers and flexible bulk containers. Rigid containers are typically made from materials like plastic, metal, or composite, offering durability and stackability for transporting liquids and solids. In contrast, flexible bulk containers, often referred to as bulk bags or FIBCs, are made from woven fabric and designed for lightweight, cost-effective storage and transport of bulk materials. Both types serve diverse industries, enhancing logistics efficiency and safety.</p></p>
