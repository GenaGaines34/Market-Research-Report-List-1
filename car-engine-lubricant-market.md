<p><ul><li>Conventional Oil</li><li>Full-synthetic Oil</li><li>Synthetic-blend Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car engine lubricant market includes three main types: Conventional Oil, Full-synthetic Oil, and Synthetic-blend Oil. Conventional oil, derived from crude oil, is cost-effective and suitable for standard engines. Full-synthetic oil, chemically engineered, offers superior performance, longevity, and protection for high-performance or extreme conditions. Synthetic-blend oil combines conventional and synthetic oils, providing better protection than conventional oil at a moderate price, ideal for vehicles with moderate driving demands. Each type caters to different engine needs, balancing cost, performance, and durability.</p></p>
