<p><p>The white spirits market is segmented into three main types based on flash points. Regular flash grade spirits typically have lower flash points, making them ideal for general use in products like cleaning agents and solvents. High flash grade spirits possess a higher flash point, which makes them safer for uses requiring more volatile substances, such as in some industrial applications. Low flash grade spirits feature even lower flash points, often found in consumer products where quick evaporation is necessary, like in perfumes.</p></p>
