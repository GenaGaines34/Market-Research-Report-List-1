<p><p>The printing ink market comprises several types, each suited for specific applications. Offset inks are primarily used in offset lithography, offering excellent quality for high-volume printing. Gravure inks are designed for high-speed rotary printing, ideal for flexible packaging. Flexo inks are versatile and used in various printing technologies, particularly for labels and packaging. Screen inks are employed for specialty applications like textile and large graphics printing. Other inks include digital and eco-friendly options, catering to diverse printing needs and environmental considerations.</p></p>
