<p><strong>The Auto Catalysts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two Way Catalyst</li><li>Three Way Catalyst</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive catalyst market is primarily segmented into two types: Two-Way Catalysts and Three-Way Catalysts. Two-Way Catalysts primarily reduce harmful emissions by converting carbon monoxide (CO) and hydrocarbons (HC) into carbon dioxide (CO2) and water. In contrast, Three-Way Catalysts perform additional functions by also converting nitrogen oxides (NOx) into nitrogen (N2) and oxygen. The Three-Way Catalyst is more advanced and widely used in modern vehicles to meet stringent emission regulations, enhancing overall environmental performance.</p></p>
