<p><p>Cloud-based business analytics software can be categorized into three market types: </p><p>1. **Private Cloud**: This setup provides dedicated resources for a single organization, ensuring greater control, security, and compliance but often at a higher cost.</p><p>2. **Public Cloud**: Here, analytics services are offered over the internet by third-party providers, allowing for scalability and cost-effectiveness, but with shared resources and potential security concerns.</p><p>3. **Hybrid Cloud**: This model combines both private and public clouds, allowing businesses to leverage flexibility while maintaining sensitive data on private infrastructure.</p></p>
