<p><strong>The Surface Protective Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Adhesive-free</li><li>Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Surface Protective Films market is categorized into two main types: adhesive-free films and adhesive films. Adhesive-free films rely on static cling or mechanical properties to adhere to surfaces, making them easy to apply and remove without leaving residue. In contrast, adhesive films utilize adhesive layers for secure application, providing robust protection against scratches, dust, and other contaminants. Each type caters to different needs, with adhesive-free films often preferred for temporary protection, while adhesive films are favored for long-lasting applications.</p></p>
