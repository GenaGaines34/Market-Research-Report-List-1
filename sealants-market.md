<p><strong>The Sealants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-borne</li><li>Solvent-borne</li><li>Reactive</li><li>Hot-melts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sealants are categorized into four primary types based on their formulation and application. Water-borne sealants are environmentally friendly and easy to clean up, making them suitable for indoor use. Solvent-borne sealants offer excellent adhesion and durability but may emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Reactive sealants undergo chemical changes during curing, providing strong bonds. Hot-melt sealants are thermoplastic adhesives that remain solid at room temperature, ideal for rapid assembly and packaging applications. Each type serves unique industry needs and applications.</p></p>
