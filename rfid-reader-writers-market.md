<p><p>The RFID reader-writer market is segmented into four main types: LF (Low Frequency), HF (High Frequency), UHF (Ultra High Frequency), and MW (Microwave) RFID reader-writers. LF RFID operates at 125 kHz and is suited for applications like animal tracking; HF RFID at 13.56 MHz is commonly used for access control and smart cards. UHF RFID, ranging from 300 MHz to 3 GHz, offers longer read ranges and is ideal for inventory management. MW RFID is less common and operates at higher frequencies for specialized applications.</p></p>
