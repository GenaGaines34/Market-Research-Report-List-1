<p><strong>The Thermal Imaging Scopes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Un-Cooled Thermal Imaging</li><li>Cryogenically Cooled Thermal Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal imaging scopes are categorized into two main types: un-cooled thermal imaging and cryogenically cooled thermal imaging. Un-cooled thermal imaging uses sensors that operate at ambient temperatures, making them more compact and cost-effective, suited for a range of applications from hunting to surveillance. In contrast, cryogenically cooled thermal imaging employs cooling systems to enhance image clarity and sensitivity, requiring more complex setups and offering superior performance in extreme conditions. These scopes serve distinct needs across military, security, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
