<p><p>The wood composite panel market comprises several types, including hardboard, MDF (Medium Density Fiberboard), particleboard, and others. Hardboard is a dense and durable panel made by compressing wood fibers, often used for furniture and flooring. MDF offers versatility and smooth surfaces, ideal for cabinetry and decorative applications. Particleboard, made from wood chips and adhesive, is cost-effective and commonly used for furniture and shelving. Other composite materials may include plywood and oriented strand board, catering to specific needs in construction and design.</p></p>
