<p><p>The contact lenses market is categorized into three main types: rigid, hybrid, and soft contact lenses. Rigid contact lenses, also known as gas permeable lenses, offer clear vision and durability but may require an adjustment period. Hybrid contact lenses combine the best features of rigid and soft lenses, providing a comfortable fit with clear vision. Soft contact lenses, widely popular, are made of flexible materials, ensuring comfort and ease of use for various vision correction needs.</p></p>
