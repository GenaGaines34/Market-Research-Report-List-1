<p><strong>The Acrylic Sheet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast Acrylic Sheet</li><li>Extruded Acrylic Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acrylic sheet market is primarily divided into two types: cast acrylic sheets and extruded acrylic sheets. Cast acrylic sheets are produced by pouring liquid acrylic into molds, resulting in high optical clarity and superior weather resistance, making them ideal for applications demanding durability and aesthetics. Extruded acrylic sheets, on the other hand, are produced through continuous casting and are generally less expensive, offering good clarity and consistent thickness, often used in displays, signage, and protective screens.</p></p>
