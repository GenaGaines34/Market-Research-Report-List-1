<p><p>The supply voltage supervisor market can be categorized into three main types: single, dual, and other market configurations. Single supply voltage supervisors monitor a single voltage level, ensuring it stays within specified limits for enhanced system reliability. Dual supply voltage supervisors, on the other hand, can monitor two voltage levels simultaneously, making them ideal for complex applications that require oversight of multiple power sources. The 'other' category includes advanced supervisors that may feature additional functionalities, such as integrated reset functions or monitoring of environmental conditions.</p></p>
