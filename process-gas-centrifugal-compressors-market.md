<p><p>Process gas centrifugal compressors are categorized into single-stage and multi-stage types based on their design and performance characteristics. Single-stage compressors utilize a single rotor to compress gas to a moderate pressure, offering simplicity and efficiency for lower pressure applications. Conversely, multi-stage compressors consist of multiple rotors arranged in stages, enabling higher pressure outputs and improving overall efficiency for demanding industrial processes. The choice between these types depends on the specific pressure, flow rate, and application requirements in various industries.</p></p>
