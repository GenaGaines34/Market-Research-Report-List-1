<p><ul><li>Anti-Lock Braking System (ABS)</li><li>Electronic Stability Control (ESC)</li><li>Electronic Brake Distribution (EBD)</li><li>Traction Control System (TSC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive brake manufacturing market encompasses various advanced systems enhancing vehicle safety and performance. Anti-Lock Braking System (ABS) prevents wheel lock-up during braking, maintaining steering ability. Electronic Stability Control (ESC) enhances vehicle stability by detecting and reducing loss of traction. Electronic Brake Distribution (EBD) optimizes brake force across all wheels based on load conditions. Traction Control System (TSC) prevents wheel spin during acceleration, ensuring optimal grip on slippery surfaces. Together, these technologies significantly improve vehicle control and safety.</p></p>
