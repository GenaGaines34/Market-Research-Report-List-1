<p><p>The portable Bluetooth speakers market can be categorized into three types: AC/DC Bluetooth speakers, AC-only Bluetooth speakers, and DC-only Bluetooth speakers. AC/DC Bluetooth speakers are versatile, functioning with both direct power and batteries, suitable for home or outdoor use.
