<p><strong>The Drop Forging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Die Drop Forging</li><li>Closed Die Drop Forging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drop forging market is primarily categorized into open die and closed die forging. Open die drop forging involves shaping metal between two flat dies, allowing for larger parts and customized shapes but requiring more manual adjustment. In contrast, closed die drop forging uses shaped dies that enclose the metal, producing more intricate designs and tighter tolerances, ideal for mass production of components. Both methods serve different industries, including automotive and aerospace, each offering unique advantages based on application needs.</p></p>
