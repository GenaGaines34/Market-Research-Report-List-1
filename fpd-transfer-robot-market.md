<p><p>The FPD (Flat Panel Display) Transfer Robot market primarily includes two types: Atmospheric Robots and Vacuum Robots. Atmospheric Robots operate in standard environmental conditions, utilizing mechanical systems to handle and transfer flat panels. They are typically used in controlled settings where exposure to dust and contaminants is minimized. Vacuum Robots, on the other hand, utilize suction mechanisms to grip and transport delicate displays securely, making them ideal for environments requiring a high level of sensitivity and protection from scratches or damage.</p></p>
