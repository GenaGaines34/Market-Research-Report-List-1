<p><ul><li>Photon Detection</li><li>Thermal Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal infrared sensor market is primarily categorized into photon detection and thermal detection types. Photon detection sensors, such as those using quantum well structures, detect infrared radiation by measuring the energy of individual photons, offering high sensitivity and speed. In contrast, thermal detection sensors, like thermopiles and bolometers, measure the heat emitted by objects, providing broader wavelength coverage but generally slower response times. Both types are essential for applications in security, environmental monitoring, and industrial processes.</p></p>
