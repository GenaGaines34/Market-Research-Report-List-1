<p><p>The pharmaceutical hot melt extrusion equipment market comprises various types, including RAM extruders, radial screen equipment, and roll and screw extruders. RAM extruders utilize a reciprocating action to provide consistent mixing and homogenization of formulations. Radial screen equipment features screens that optimize material flow during extrusion, enhancing product uniformity. Roll and screw extruders combine rolling and screw elements for efficient compounding and processing. Each type plays a critical role in improving drug solubility and bioavailability in pharmaceutical applications.</p></p>
