EDGE (Enhanced Data rates for GSM Evolution) further improves data rates, facilitating more efficient transmission of information. These modules empower a wide range of IoT applications, including smart metering, wearable devices, and asset tracking.</p><p>The market for GSM GPRS and EDGE modules is witnessing significant growth, mainly driven by the increasing demand for IoT solutions across various industries, such as agriculture, healthcare, and logistics.
