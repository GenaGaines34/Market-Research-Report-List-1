<p><p>The ice machine market can be divided into two primary types: soft ice cream machines and hard ice cream machines. Soft ice cream machines produce a smoother, creamier texture ideal for serving immediately, commonly found in restaurants and kiosks. In contrast, hard ice cream machines create firmer ice cream that requires freezing before serving, making them suitable for commercial ice cream parlors. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and operational needs, influencing their design and functionality in the market.</p></p>
