<p><strong>The HVAC Terminal Units Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Duct</li><li>Dual Duct</li><li>Fan Powered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HVAC terminal units regulate air distribution in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems. Single duct systems supply conditioned air through one duct and typically use volume control dampers for temperature regulation. Dual duct systems feature two separate ducts, one for hot air and another for cold air, allowing for precise temperature control in varying spaces. Fan-powered terminal units incorporate fans to enhance airflow, providing greater flexibility and efficiency in distributing conditioned air across larger areas or against high resistance.</p></p>
