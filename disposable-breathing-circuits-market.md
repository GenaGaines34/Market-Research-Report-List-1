<p><p>The disposable breathing circuits market includes three primary types: </p><p>1. **Open Breathing Circuits** allow ambient air mixing with the delivered gas, primarily used in emergency settings. </p><p>2. **Semi-Open Breathing Circuits** feature a partial rebreathing mechanism, balancing gas exchange and efficiency, commonly used in anesthesia. </p><p>3. **Closed Breathing Circuits** are fully enclosed systems that minimize gas loss, enhancing safety and efficiency, often employed in critical care environments. Each type serves unique clinical needs and operational efficiencies.</p></p>
