<p><strong>The Microneedle Flu Vaccine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Microneedle</li><li>Hollow Microneedle</li><li>Coated Microneedle</li><li>Dissolving Microneedle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microneedle flu vaccine market encompasses several types: </p><p>1. **Solid Microneedles** are sharp, solid structures designed to penetrate the skin and deliver vaccines directly into the dermal layer.</p><p>2. **Hollow Microneedles** contain a small lumen for liquid vaccine delivery, allowing for a more controlled release.</p><p>3. **Coated Microneedles** are coated with vaccine formulations, which dissolve upon insertion, delivering the vaccine as the microneedle dissolves.</p><p>4. **Dissolving Microneedles** consist of biodegradable materials that dissolve in the skin, releasing the vaccine without leaving any residue.</p></p>
