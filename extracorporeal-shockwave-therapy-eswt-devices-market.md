<p><p>Extracorporeal Shockwave Therapy (ESWT) devices are categorized into three main market types: table-top/portable ESWT devices, hand-push type ESWT devices, and stationary ESWT units. Table-top/portable devices offer flexibility and mobility, making them suitable for clinics with limited space or for use in home care settings. Hand-push type devices are designed for manual operation, allowing practitioners precise control during treatment. Stationary units are typically larger systems installed in healthcare facilities, providing high-intensity shockwave therapies for a range of musculoskeletal conditions.</p></p>
