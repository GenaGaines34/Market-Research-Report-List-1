<p><p>The infrared radiation (IR) emitter and receiver market is segmented into four main types: Long Wave IR (LWIR), Short Wave IR (SWIR), Far Wave IR (FWIR), and Mid Wave IR (MWIR). LWIR operates in the 8-14 micrometers range, ideal for thermal imaging. SWIR covers 0.9-1.7 micrometers, suitable for agricultural and industrial applications. MWIR operates between 3-5 micrometers, used in military and surveillance. FWIR encompasses wavelengths longer than LWIR, often associated with specialized scientific and industrial uses, ensuring diverse applications across sectors.</p></p>
