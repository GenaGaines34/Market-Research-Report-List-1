<p><p>Pyrolytic Graphite (PG) is a high-purity, highly crystalline form of graphite produced through pyrolysis. The market for PG is segmented into two main types: sheet and powder. Sheet PG is utilized for applications requiring efficient thermal management, such as in electronics or as neutron reflectors in nuclear reactors, due to its excellent thermal conductivity. Powder PG, on the other hand, is employed in various industrial applications, including lubricants and composites, benefiting from its lubricating properties and chemical stability.</p></p>
