<p><p>The live rock market comprises primarily two types: aquaculture live rock and marine natural rock. Aquaculture live rock is cultivated in controlled environments, ensuring sustainability and reduced environmental impact, often featuring a rich biodiversity of beneficial organisms. Conversely, marine natural rock is harvested from wild reefs, which can lead to ecological concerns due to overharvesting. Both types serve as crucial components in marine aquariums, providing habitats for aquatic life and contributing to the biological filtration in reef systems.</p></p>
