<p><p>The pressure sensors market comprises various types, primarily including MEMS pressure sensors, pressure transducers, and pressure transmitters. MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) pressure sensors are compact and highly sensitive, ideal for consumer electronics and automotive applications. Pressure transducers convert pressure into an electrical signal, commonly used in industrial processes. Pressure transmitters, on the other hand, provide more advanced signal conditioning and are utilized in complex systems for accurate monitoring. Other market types include piezoresistive and capacitive sensors, serving diverse applications across industries.</p></p>
