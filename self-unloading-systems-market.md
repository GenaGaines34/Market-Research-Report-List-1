<p><p>Self-unloading systems are critical for efficient bulk material handling. The pneumatic system utilizes air pressure to transport materials through pipelines, offering flexibility and reduced contamination. Grab rope trolleys employ a grab bucket mechanism to lift and deposit loads, ideal for uneven surfaces. Vertical screw systems use helical screws to move materials vertically, suitable for granular substances. Elevator bucket chains consist of buckets attached to a chain, efficiently lifting bulk materials to higher elevations, making them versatile for various industrial applications.</p></p>
