<p><p>Nickel Cobalt Manganese (NCM) Lithium Acid batteries are categorized into three primary types based on their specific compositions: NCM-333, NCM-423, and NCM-523. NCM-333 contains equal parts of Nickel, Cobalt, and Manganese, balancing energy density and stability. NCM-423 emphasizes more Nickel for higher capacity, while still retaining thermal stability. NCM-523 further enhances this by increasing Nickel content, maximizing energy density and power output, making it ideal for applications requiring long-lasting performance and efficiency in electric vehicles and energy storage systems.</p></p>
