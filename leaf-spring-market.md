<p><strong>The Leaf Spring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-Leaf Spring</li><li>Mono-Leaf Spring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The leaf spring market primarily consists of multi-leaf and mono-leaf spring types, each suited for different vehicle applications. Multi-leaf springs, made from several stacked leaves, offer enhanced load capacity, durability, and stability, making them ideal for heavy-duty vehicles. In contrast, mono-leaf springs, consisting of a single curved leaf, provide a lighter weight option with improved ride quality and flexibility, typically used in lighter vehicles and passenger cars. Both types cater to diverse automotive needs based on performance and weight considerations.</p></p>
