<p><p>Isolation Service Manifolds are classified based on the number of valves they incorporate, which directly affects their functionality in managing and isolating fluid flow in various applications. A 2-valve manifold typically facilitates basic isolation, allowing flow control in simple systems. The 3-valve manifold enables more versatility with an additional venting option, useful for pressure measurements. Meanwhile, a 5-valve manifold offers comprehensive functionality, including more precise control and additional connections for pressure transmitters, making it ideal for complex instrumentation setups.</p></p>
