<p><p>The Electronic Toll Collection (ETC) Systems market comprises several types, including RFID-based systems that use radio-frequency identification for automatic toll charging, and DSRC-based systems that utilize dedicated short-range communication for real-time vehicle identification. Video analytic systems employ cameras and advanced imaging technology to monitor and charge tolls based on license plate recognition. GNSS/GPS-based systems leverage satellite positioning to track vehicle locations for toll calculation, offering flexibility and accuracy in toll management across diverse infrastructures.</p></p>
