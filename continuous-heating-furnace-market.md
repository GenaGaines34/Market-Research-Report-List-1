<p><strong>The Continuous Heating Furnace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary-Hearth Type</li><li>Straight-Chamber Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Continuous Heating Furnace market comprises two primary types: Rotary-Hearth and Straight-Chamber. Rotary-Hearth type features a rotating platform that allows materials to be heated uniformly while being processed, making it efficient for high-volume applications. In contrast, the Straight-Chamber type utilizes a linear design where items pass through a confined chamber, enabling consistent temperature control for various materials. Both types are essential in industries like metals and ceramics, supporting continuous production and improving operational efficiency.</p></p>
