<p><p>The Industrial Machine Vision System market is primarily categorized into two types: PC-Based Vision Systems and Smart Camera-Based Vision Systems. PC-Based Vision Systems utilize powerful computers for processing high-resolution images, ideal for complex tasks and large-scale applications. In contrast, Smart Camera-Based Vision Systems integrate processing capabilities directly within the camera, offering compactness and ease of installation for simpler tasks. Both systems enhance automation, improve quality control, and optimize production processes across various industries.</p></p>
