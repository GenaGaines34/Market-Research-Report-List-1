<p><p>The automotive mechanical fan market includes various types, primarily clutch fans, flex fans, and others. Clutch fans utilize a thermal or electronic clutch to engage and disengage the fan based on engine temperature, optimizing airflow and efficiency. Flex fans feature blades that pivot, adjusting their angle to improve airflow at different engine speeds. Other types may include fixed fans or specialized designs for specific applications. Each type offers distinct advantages in cooling performance and energy efficiency, catering to diverse vehicle needs.</p></p>
