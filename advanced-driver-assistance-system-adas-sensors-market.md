Radar sensors utilize radio waves to detect distances and speeds, enhancing adaptive cruise control and collision avoidance. Lidar sensors employ laser technology to create detailed 3D maps of the environment, enabling precise object detection and recognition, crucial for autonomous driving and advanced safety features.</p></p>
