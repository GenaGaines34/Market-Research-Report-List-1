<p><p>The silicon-based fingerprint sensor market is primarily divided into two types: touch type and slide type. Touch type sensors capture fingerprints through direct contact, offering quick access to devices with high accuracy and user convenience. In contrast, slide type sensors require users to swipe their finger across the sensor surface, allowing for longer fingerprints to be captured. Both types are widely used in consumer electronics, security systems, and payment solutions, offering varying advantages in terms of usability and integration.</p></p>
