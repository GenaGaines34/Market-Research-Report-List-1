<p><p>The O-ring gripper market encompasses two primary types: parallel grippers and angular grippers. Parallel grippers maintain consistent jaw separation, effectively providing a secure grip on objects of varying sizes, making them ideal for high-precision applications. Angular grippers, on the other hand, feature jaws that move in an angular motion, enabling them to grasp components at an angle. Both types are widely used in automation and robotics, enhancing operational efficiency and accuracy in handling diverse materials and shapes.</p></p>
