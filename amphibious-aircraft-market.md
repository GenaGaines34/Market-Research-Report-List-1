<p><p>The amphibious aircraft market is divided into two main types: fixed-wing and rotary-wing aircraft. Fixed-wing amphibious aircraft are designed for both land and water operations, featuring traditional wings and horizontal stabilizers for extended range and speed. In contrast, rotary-wing amphibious aircraft, or amphibious helicopters, utilize rotor systems to enable vertical takeoff and landing on water and land. Both types serve various roles, including search and rescue, tourism, and cargo transport, capitalizing on their versatility in diverse environments.</p></p>
