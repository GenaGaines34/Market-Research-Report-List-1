<p><strong>The Automated Optical Metrology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA)</li><li>Enzyme Linked Fluorescent Immunoassay (ELFA)</li><li>Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA)</li><li>Radioimmunoassay (RIA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated Optical Metrology encompasses several key market types in immunodiagnostics. Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA) utilizes luminescent reactions for sensitive detection. Enzyme Linked Fluorescent Immunoassay (ELFA) employs fluorescent markers, enhancing specificity and sensitivity. Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) uses enzyme-linked antibodies for colorimetric detection, enabling quantitative analysis of antigens. Radioimmunoassay (RIA) relies on radioactively labeled antibodies, providing high sensitivity but with safety concerns. Each method offers distinct advantages for various applications in clinical and research settings.</p></p>
