<p><strong>The Wavelength Selective Switch WSS Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LCOS based Wavelength Selective Switch (WSS) Modules</li><li>MEMS based Wavelength Selective Switch (WSS) Modules</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wavelength Selective Switch (WSS) modules are critical components in optical networks, enabling dynamic wavelength routing and management. LCOS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon) based WSS modules utilize liquid crystal technology for precise wavelength selection, offering high performance and versatility, ideal for dense wavelength division multiplexing. In contrast, MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) based WSS modules leverage micro-machined mirrors for rapid light beam switching, providing scalability and cost-effectiveness. Both technologies cater to diverse applications in telecommunications and data centers, enhancing network efficiency and flexibility.</p></p>
