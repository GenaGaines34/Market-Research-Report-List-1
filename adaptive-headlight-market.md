<p><p>The adaptive headlight market includes three main types: halogen, xenon, and LED headlights. Halogen headlights are traditional and cost-effective, providing decent illumination but with limited efficiency. Xenon headlights, also known as HID, offer brighter light output and improved visibility but at a higher cost. LED headlights are the most advanced, providing superior brightness, energy efficiency, and longevity. Each type adapts lighting patterns based on driving conditions, enhancing safety and comfort, leading to diverse applications across various vehicle segments.</p></p>
