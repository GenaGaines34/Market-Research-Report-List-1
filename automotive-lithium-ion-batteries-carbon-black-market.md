<p><p>The automotive lithium-ion batteries carbon black market includes three primary types: Lamp Black, Acetylene Black, and Gas Black. Lamp Black, produced from the incomplete combustion of hydrocarbons, offers excellent conductivity and is used for enhancing battery performance. Acetylene Black, derived from acetylene gas, exhibits high purity and superior electrical conductivity, making it ideal for high-performance applications. Gas Black, obtained from the thermal decomposition of hydrocarbons, provides good electrical properties and is often utilized for standard battery manufacturing, catering to various automotive applications.</p></p>
