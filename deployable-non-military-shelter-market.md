<p><p>The Deployable Non-Military Shelter market is divided into two primary types: soft wall and hard wall shelters. Soft wall shelters are lightweight, flexible structures often made from fabric or canvas, allowing for easy transport and quick setup. They are suitable for temporary uses in various environments. Conversely, hard wall shelters consist of rigid materials like metal or composite panels, offering enhanced durability and security. These are ideal for more permanent installations, providing greater protection against harsh conditions and risks.</p></p>
