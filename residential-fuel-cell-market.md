<p><strong>The Residential Fuel Cell Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PEM</li><li>SOFC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential fuel cell market primarily consists of two types: Proton Exchange Membrane (PEM) fuel cells and Solid Oxide Fuel Cells (SOFC). PEM fuel cells are known for their quick start-up times and efficiency at lower temperatures, making them suitable for residential applications requiring immediate power. In contrast, SOFCs operate at higher temperatures and offer greater efficiency for continuous energy generation over longer periods, making them ideal for larger homes or continuous energy needs. Both types provide clean, reliable energy solutions for residential use.</p></p>
