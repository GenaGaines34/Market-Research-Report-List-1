<p><p>Same Day Delivery markets can be categorized into two main types: Intra-city Express and City-city Express. Intra-city Express focuses on delivering goods within a single city, ensuring rapid transit to meet urgent customer demands. This typically involves local couriers or delivery networks. Conversely, City-city Express operates between different cities, providing fast delivery services for time-sensitive packages that need to cover longer distances, often requiring coordination among regional logistics networks to ensure prompt arrival.</p></p>
