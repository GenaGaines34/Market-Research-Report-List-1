<p><p>The non-isolated DC-DC converter market is categorized into step-down, step-up, and other types. Step-down converters, or buck converters, reduce voltage while increasing current, making them ideal for battery-powered devices. Step-up converters, or boost converters, increase voltage at the expense of current, suitable for applications requiring higher voltage levels. Other types may include buck-boost converters, which can both step up and step down the voltage, providing versatile power management solutions for various electronics and industrial applications.</p></p>
