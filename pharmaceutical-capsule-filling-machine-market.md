<p><p>The pharmaceutical capsule filling machine market is categorized into three types: fully automatic, semi-automatic, and manual machines. Fully automatic machines offer high-speed production and minimal human intervention, making them ideal for large-scale operations. Semi-automatic machines require some manual input but still streamline the filling process, suitable for medium-scale production. Manual machines rely heavily on operator skills and are best for small-scale or specialized batch production, offering flexibility and lower initial investment costs. Each type serves distinct production needs in the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
