<p><strong>The Float Zone Silicon Wafers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>P-Type</li><li>N-Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Float zone silicon wafers are categorized into P-type and N-type based on the doping elements used to create holes or electrons as charge carriers. P-type wafers are doped with elements like boron, resulting in a positive charge carrier concentration, making them suitable for applications requiring high hole mobility. In contrast, N-type wafers are doped with elements such as phosphorus, leading to an abundance of electrons, ideal for applications demanding high electron mobility. Together, they cater to the semiconductor and photovoltaic industries.</p></p>
