<p><strong>The Luminescent Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluorescent Paint</li><li>Phosphorescent Paint</li><li>Radio Luminescent Paint</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The luminescent paint market comprises three main types: fluorescent, phosphorescent, and radio luminescent paints. Fluorescent paint absorbs light and re-emits it almost instantly, creating a vibrant glow under UV light. Phosphorescent paint absorbs light and releases it slowly, providing a lasting afterglow in the dark. Radio luminescent paint contains radioactive materials, emitting light continuously without external light sources. Each type serves unique applications, including safety signage, artistic endeavors, and decorative features, catering to both aesthetic and functional needs.</p></p>
