<p><p>The handheld intraoral scanner market includes two main types: wired and wireless scanners. Wired scanners connect directly to a computer or processing unit, providing stable data transmission and often enhanced accuracy. They tend to be more cost-effective but can limit mobility during procedures. In contrast, wireless scanners operate via Bluetooth or Wi-Fi, offering greater flexibility and ease of use for dental professionals. While they allow for more comfortable patient interaction, they may come with higher costs and potential connectivity issues.</p></p>
