<p><p>The Capsule Endoscopy Robot market includes two main types: Internally Driven Type and Magnetron Type. The Internally Driven Type utilizes motorized movement within the capsule to navigate the gastrointestinal tract, enabling high-resolution imaging. In contrast, the Magnetron Type relies on external magnetic fields to guide the capsule's movement, offering precise control over its path. Both types enhance diagnostic capabilities in gastroenterology by providing non-invasive, real-time visualization, thereby improving patient outcomes and facilitating better clinical decision-making.</p></p>
