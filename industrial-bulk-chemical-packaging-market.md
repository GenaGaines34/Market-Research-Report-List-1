<p><p>The industrial bulk chemical packaging market encompasses various types designed for handling large volumes of chemicals. Drums are cylindrical containers, typically made of steel or plastic, suitable for storing liquids or powders. Flexitanks are flexible bags used in shipping bulk liquids, offering cost-effective solutions for transportation. Intermediate Bulk Containers (IBCs) are standardized plastic or metal tanks ideal for storing and transporting bulk materials, combining strength with volume efficiency. Other packaging options may include totes, bulk bags, or specialized containers tailored for specific industries.</p></p>
