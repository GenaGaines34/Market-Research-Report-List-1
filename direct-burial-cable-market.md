<p><strong>The Direct Burial Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid</li><li>Twisted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct burial cables are designed for underground installation without additional protective conduits. The market includes solid and twisted types. Solid cables consist of a single, continuous conductor, providing lower resistance and better conductivity, making them suitable for long-distance applications. Twisted cables feature pairs of insulated conductors twisted together, which helps reduce electromagnetic interference and enhance signal quality. Each type serves specific needs, with solid cables often favored for power transmission and twisted cables commonly used for communication purposes.</p></p>
