<p><p>Connected Vehicle Gateway (CVG) facilitates communication between vehicle systems and external networks, enabling data exchange for enhanced connectivity and safety. Market types include the Central Gateway, serving as the primary communication hub within a vehicle for various internal systems, and the LAN Gateway, which manages data transmission over local area networks to connect external devices. Both gateways enable seamless interaction among vehicle components and with external services, supporting advanced features like telematics, infotainment, and vehicle-to-everything (V2X) communication.</p></p>
