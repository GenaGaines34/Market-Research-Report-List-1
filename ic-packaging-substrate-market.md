<p><p>The IC packaging substrate market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **WB CSP (Wafer-Level Chip Scale Package)** offers small form factors and low overhead.</p><p>2. **FC BGA (Flip Chip Ball Grid Array)** enables high-density connections and efficient heat dissipation.</p><p>3. **FC CSP (Flip Chip Chip Scale Package)** focuses on compact designs with reduced manufacturing costs.</p><p>4. **PBGA (Plastic Ball Grid Array)** is cost-effective for various applications.</p><p>5. **SiP (System in Package)** integrates multiple components in a single module.</p><p>6. **BOC (Bump on Chip)** supports advanced packaging technologies.</p><p>7. **Others** encompass emerging solutions and niche applications within the market.</p></p>
