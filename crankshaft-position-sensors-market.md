<p><strong>The Crankshaft Position Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Position Sensor</li><li>Rotary Position Sensor</li><li>Proximity Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The crankshaft position sensors market comprises three main types: linear position sensors, rotary position sensors, and proximity sensors. Linear position sensors detect the straight-line position of the crankshaft, crucial for precise timing in engine management. Rotary position sensors measure the angle and speed of the crankshaft's rotation, assisting in fuel injection and ignition timing. Proximity sensors monitor the crankshaft's position without direct contact, enhancing reliability and longevity. Together, these sensors are essential for optimizing engine performance and efficiency.</p></p>
