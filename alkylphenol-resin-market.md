<p><p>The alkylphenol resin market is categorized into two main types: reactive and non-reactive. Reactive alkylphenol resins are designed to undergo chemical reactions, making them suitable for applications like adhesives and coatings where strong bonding is essential. Non-reactive alkylphenol resins, on the other hand, do not participate in chemical reactions and are primarily used as additives to enhance properties such as durability and chemical resistance in products like plastics and varnishes. Both types serve distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
