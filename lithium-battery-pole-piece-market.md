<p><ul><li>Cathode Pole Piece</li><li>Anode Pole Piece</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lithium battery pole piece market is segmented into two primary types: cathode pole pieces and anode pole pieces. Cathode pole pieces are responsible for the positive charge, typically made from lithium compounds, which store energy during discharge. In contrast, anode pole pieces serve as the negative charge, often composed of materials like graphite, enabling energy absorption during charging. Together, these components are crucial for the efficiency and performance of lithium-ion batteries, used in various applications from electric vehicles to consumer electronics.</p></p>
