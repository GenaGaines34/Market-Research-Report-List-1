<p><p>The outdoor storage sheds market is categorized into three primary types: wood, metal, and plastic. Wooden sheds offer aesthetic appeal and natural insulation, making them popular for traditional settings, but may require regular maintenance. Metal sheds are durable, fire-resistant, and secure, suitable for harsher climates, though they may be prone to rust if not treated. Plastic sheds are lightweight, rot-resistant, and low-maintenance, making them ideal for outdoor storage in various weather conditions, though they may lack the same structural strength as wood or metal.</p></p>
