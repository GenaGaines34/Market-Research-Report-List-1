<p><p>The composite architectural membrane market is categorized into three types: transparent, semi-transparent, and opaque. Transparent membranes allow maximum light transmission, making them ideal for applications requiring visibility and aesthetics. Semi-transparent membranes provide some light filtering while maintaining privacy and diffusing harsh sunlight, suitable for energy-efficient buildings. Opaque membranes block light entirely, offering privacy and protection from external elements, often used in structural applications where insulation and weather resistance are priorities. Each type serves distinct architectural needs.</p></p>
