<p><ul><li>Densified Silica Fume</li><li>Semi Densified Silica Fume</li><li>Undensified Silica Fume</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrafine silica fume, or microsilica, is categorized into three market types based on density and processing. Densified silica fume is compacted for easy handling and greater flowability, making it ideal for high-performance concrete. Semi densified silica fume has moderate compaction, offering a balance between flowability and silica content. Undensified silica fume retains its original, fluffy state, optimizing surface area for applications needing high reactivity. These variations cater to specific requirements in construction, aerospace, and other industries.</p></p>
