<p><ul><li>Singlemode</li><li>Multimode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Backplane Connectors market comprises two main types: Singlemode and Multimode. Singlemode connectors are designed for long-distance communication, utilizing a single light path to minimize signal loss, making them ideal for telecommunications and data centers requiring high bandwidth over extended ranges. In contrast, Multimode connectors support shorter distances, using multiple light paths to transmit data, which is advantageous for local area networks and data transfers within buildings. Both types are essential for efficient data transmission in various applications.</p></p>
