<p><p>The office headsets market comprises three main types: on-ear, over-ear, and in-ear. On-ear headsets rest gently on the ears, offering portability and comfort for short to medium use. Over-ear headsets envelop the entire ear, providing superior sound quality and noise isolation, ideal for long work sessions. In-ear headsets fit snugly inside the ear canal, offering a discreet design and portability, making them suitable for mobile workers or multitaskers. Each type caters to different user preferences and workplace environments.</p></p>
