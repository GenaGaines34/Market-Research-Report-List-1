<p><strong>The CMP Diamond Disks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional CMP Diamond Pad Disks</li><li>CVD Diamond CMP Pad Disks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CMP (Chemical Mechanical Polishing) diamond disks market primarily consists of two types: conventional CMP diamond pad disks and CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition) diamond CMP pad disks. Conventional CMP diamond pad disks are typically made from synthetic diamond particles embedded in a resin or polymer matrix, offering good performance for various semiconductor applications. In contrast, CVD diamond CMP pad disks are produced through chemical vapor deposition, providing superior hardness, thermal stability, and longer lifespan, making them ideal for advanced semiconductor fabrication processes.</p></p>
