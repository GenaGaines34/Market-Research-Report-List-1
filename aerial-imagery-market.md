<p><p>The aerial imagery market is categorized into three types: Low Oblique Imagery, High Oblique Imagery, and Vertical Imagery. Low Oblique Imagery captures scenes with a slight angle, providing a perspective that highlights both the ground and the horizon, ideal for showcasing features like buildings. High Oblique Imagery has a steeper angle, emphasizing landscape and topography while reducing visible details. Vertical Imagery is captured directly above, offering accurate representations of land features, crucial for mapping and surveying applications.</p></p>
