<p><p>The IF Steel market is categorized into Soft Steel and High Strength Deep Drawing Steel. Soft Steel is characterized by its ductility and malleability, making it ideal for forming processes where flexibility is essential. In contrast, High Strength Deep Drawing Steel boasts enhanced tensile strength, enabling it to withstand significant deformation during processes like deep drawing without fracturing. Both types are pivotal in automotive and manufacturing sectors, catering to varying application needs based on strength, formability, and performance requirements.</p></p>
