<p><p>The bakery dough conditioners market includes two primary types: powders and fluids. Powdered conditioners are convenient for easy mixing into dry ingredients, enhancing shelf life and texture. Both types are utilized by bakers to optimize dough performance and product quality, catering to diverse baking applications and consumer preferences.</p></p>
