<p><p>The performance coating market is segmented into water-based, solvent-based, and other types. Solvent-based coatings utilize organic solvents, providing durability and excellent adhesion but may pose higher environmental concerns. The "others" category includes specialty coatings, such as powder and UV-curable coatings, which cater to specific applications like industrial and automotive sectors. Each type serves distinct needs based on performance, environmental impact, and application requirements.</p></p>
