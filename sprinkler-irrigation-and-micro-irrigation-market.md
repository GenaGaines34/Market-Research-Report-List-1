<p><p>Sprinkler and micro irrigation systems are essential for efficient water management in agriculture and landscaping. Sprinklers include spray and rotor types that distribute water over large areas, while micro irrigation focuses on targeted watering. Drippers deliver precise amounts directly to plant roots, micro sprinklers spray water in a fine mist, and impact sprinklers use a rotating mechanism for broader coverage. Gun sprinklers release water over greater distances, making them ideal for large fields. Together, these systems enhance crop yield and conserve water.</p></p>
