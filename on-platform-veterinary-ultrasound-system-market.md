<p><p>The on-platform veterinary ultrasound system market is segmented into black and white ultrasound and color ultrasound. Black and white ultrasound, also known as B-mode imaging, provides grayscale images that are effective for assessing animal anatomy and detecting abnormalities. Color ultrasound, on the other hand, integrates Doppler technology, enabling real-time visualization of blood flow and enhancing diagnostic capabilities for conditions like cardiac issues. Both types serve critical roles in veterinary diagnostics, supporting improved animal health outcomes.</p></p>
