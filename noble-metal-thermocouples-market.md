<p><p>Noble metal thermocouples, known for their high accuracy and stability at elevated temperatures, include R, S, and B types. R-type thermocouples, made from platinum-rhodium, are used in various industrial applications due to their reliability. S-type thermocouples, also platinum-rhodium, are ideal for high-temperature settings, often in laboratory environments. B-type thermocouples, which utilize platinum-rhodium alloys, excel in extreme temperatures, making them suitable for applications like molten metal measurements. Each type serves specific needs in high-temperature sensing.</p></p>
