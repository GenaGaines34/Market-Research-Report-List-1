<p><p>The tyre cord fabrics market serves two main applications: bias tires and radial tires (semi-steel). Bias tires feature layers of fabric running diagonally, providing flexibility and a smoother ride, making them suitable for specific vehicles like older models and some agricultural machinery. In contrast, radial tires utilize fabric layers that run perpendicular to the direction of travel, offering improved handling, fuel efficiency, and stability. The semi-steel variant combines textile and steel components for enhanced performance, catering to modern vehicles and commercial applications.</p></p>
