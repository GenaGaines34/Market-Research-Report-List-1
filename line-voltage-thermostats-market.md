<p><p>The line-voltage thermostats market primarily features two wiring types: single pole and double pole. Single pole wiring controls a single circuit, typically used with baseboard heaters, allowing simple on/off functionality. In contrast, double pole wiring manages two circuits, often needed for higher voltage systems, ensuring safer and more efficient operation of equipment like electric furnaces. Each type caters to different heating and cooling applications, influencing installation choices and energy management in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
