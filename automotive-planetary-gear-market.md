<p><ul><li>Simple Planetary Gear</li><li>Complex Planetary Gear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive planetary gear market is categorized into two main types: simple planetary gears and complex planetary gears. Simple planetary gears consist of a central sun gear, surrounding planet gears, and an outer ring gear, functioning efficiently for basic applications like automatic transmissions. In contrast, complex planetary gears incorporate additional elements such as multiple sun gears or intermediate gears, enhancing torque capacity and performance, making them suitable for advanced applications like hybrid and electric vehicles. Both types play crucial roles in modern automotive design.</p></p>
