<p><p>The oilfield drilling additives market encompasses various types that enhance drilling efficiency and safety. Dispersants improve fluid flow by preventing particle aggregation, while fluid viscosifiers increase the thickness of drilling fluids for better pressure control. Corrosion inhibitors protect equipment from degradation, and biocides reduce microbial growth to maintain fluid integrity. Surface modifiers enhance fluid stability and performance, and defoamers eliminate foam that can hinder operations. Other additives may include agents that address specific drilling challenges or improve environmental compliance.</p></p>
