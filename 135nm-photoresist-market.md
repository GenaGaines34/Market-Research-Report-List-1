<p><p>The 13.5nm photoresist market is primarily divided into two types: dry and liquid photoresists. Dry 13.5nm photoresists are typically utilized in extreme ultraviolet lithography (EUV) processes, offering precise patterning for semiconductor manufacturing. They provide advantages such as better resolution and reduced defects. In contrast, liquid 13.5nm photoresists are applied in a wet process, allowing for uniform coatings over large areas. Each type serves distinct needs in the semiconductor industry, catering to specific application requirements and manufacturing techniques.</p></p>
