Lower extremity bracing focuses on the knees, ankles, and feet, providing support for fractures, sprains, and post-surgical recovery. Spinal bracing addresses issues related to the spine, including scoliosis and post-operative care, helping to stabilize and align the vertebral column.</p></p>
