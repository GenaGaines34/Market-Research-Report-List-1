<p><ul><li>Single Lens (Single Channel)</li><li>Multi Lens (Dual Channel)</li><li>Rearview Dashboard Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart dashboard cameras come in various types, primarily categorized by lens configuration. Single lens (single channel) cameras feature one lens, capturing footage from the front of the vehicle, ideal for basic monitoring. Multi lens (dual channel) cameras include front and rear lenses, allowing comprehensive recording of both directions, enhancing safety. Rearview dashboard cameras are specifically designed to replace traditional rearview mirrors, providing a streamlined view of the rear while incorporating camera functionality for added surveillance and security.</p></p>
