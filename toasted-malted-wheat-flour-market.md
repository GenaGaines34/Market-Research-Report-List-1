<p><strong>The Toasted Malted Wheat Flour Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diastatic Toasted Malted Wheat Flour</li><li>Non-Diastatic Toasted Malted Wheat Flour</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The toasted malted wheat flour market is divided into two primary types: diastatic and non-diastatic. Diastatic toasted malted wheat flour contains active enzymes that aid in fermentation, enhancing the dough's rise and providing a distinct flavor profile, making it ideal for baking bread and pastries. Non-diastatic toasted malted wheat flour, on the other hand, lacks these enzymes and serves primarily as a flavoring and coloring agent, commonly used in brewing and specialty baked goods to impart a rich taste and aroma.</p></p>
