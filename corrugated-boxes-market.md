<p><p>The corrugated boxes market is categorized into three main types based on their construction. Single corrugated boxes consist of one layer of fluted paper sandwiched between two liners, ideal for lightweight products. Double corrugated boxes include two layers of fluted paper, providing greater strength and cushioning for heavier items. Triple corrugated boxes feature three layers of fluting, offering maximum durability for very heavy or fragile goods, making them suitable for industrial applications and long-distance shipping.</p></p>
