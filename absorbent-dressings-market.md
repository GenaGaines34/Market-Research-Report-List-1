<p><strong>The Absorbent Dressings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Adherent</li><li>Adherent</li><li>Low-Adherent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The absorbent dressings market is categorized into three main types: non-adherent, adherent, and low-adherent dressings. Non-adherent dressings prevent sticking to wounds, making them easy to remove without causing trauma. Adherent dressings firmly attach to the skin, promoting optimal healing for minor cuts and surgical sites. Low-adherent dressings strike a balance, allowing minimal adhesion while absorbing exudate. Each type serves distinct wound management needs, catering to varying levels of moisture and healing requirements.</p></p>
