Absolute refers to highly concentrated forms of bay leaf oil, extracted through solvent extraction, retaining the plant's full aroma and flavor. Concentrates are oils that have undergone distillation or other refining processes, offering a potent flavor profile. Blends combine bay leaf oil with other essential oils or carriers, creating unique fragrances and enhancing versatility for various applications in aromatherapy, culinary, and cosmetic industries.</p></p>
