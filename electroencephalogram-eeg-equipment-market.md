<p><p>The electroencephalogram (EEG) equipment market is primarily divided into two types: EEG caps and EEG computers. EEG caps are wearable devices that contain multiple electrodes embedded in a flexible fabric, allowing for easy attachment to the scalp and facilitating real-time brain activity monitoring. On the other hand, EEG computers refer to the software and hardware systems that process, analyze, and visualize the electrical activity recorded by the electrodes, enabling clinicians and researchers to interpret brain signals effectively.</p></p>
