<p><strong>The Wheel Speed Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Wheel Speed Sensor</li><li>Magnetic Electric Wheel Speed Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wheel speed sensor market is primarily categorized into Hall wheel speed sensors and magnetic electric wheel speed sensors. Hall wheel speed sensors utilize Hall-effect technology to detect rotations and provide precise speed data, making them popular in modern vehicles for anti-lock braking systems (ABS) and traction control. Conversely, magnetic electric wheel speed sensors use electromagnetic induction to generate voltage in response to wheel movement. Both types play critical roles in vehicle safety and performance by monitoring wheel speed and ensuring optimal functionality.</p></p>
