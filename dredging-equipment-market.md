<p><strong>The Dredging Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Dredger</li><li>Hydraulic Dredger</li><li>Other Dredger</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dredging equipment market comprises three main types: mechanical dredgers, hydraulic dredgers, and other dredgers. Mechanical dredgers, such as clamshells and draglines, use buckets or claws to scoop material from the seabed. Hydraulic dredgers utilize high-pressure water jets to fluidize soil, allowing it to be pumped away. Other dredgers encompass specialized equipment like airlift and environmental dredgers, designed for specific tasks or conditions. Each type plays a critical role in maintaining waterways, constructing ports, and managing sediment.</p></p>
