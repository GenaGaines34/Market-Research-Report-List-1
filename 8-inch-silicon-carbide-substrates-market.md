<p><p>The 8-inch silicon carbide substrates market comprises two main types: conductive and semi-insulating. Conductive type substrates are characterized by their ability to allow electric current to flow, making them ideal for high-power applications like power electronics and RF devices. In contrast, semi-insulating type substrates exhibit high resistivity, minimizing the leakage current and enhancing device performance in high-frequency applications. Both types are essential for advancing technologies in sectors such as telecommunications, automotive, and renewable energy, driving growth and innovation in the market.</p></p>
