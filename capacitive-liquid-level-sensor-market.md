<p><strong>The Capacitive Liquid Level Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Capacitive Liquid Level Sensor</li><li>Non-contact Capacitive Liquid Level Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The capacitive liquid level sensor market is divided into two main types: contact and non-contact capacitive liquid level sensors. Contact capacitive sensors directly interact with the liquid, measuring changes in capacitance based on the liquid's dielectric properties. In contrast, non-contact capacitive sensors detect liquid levels without physical contact, utilizing electromagnetic fields. Both types offer advantages in various applications, such as industrial, automotive, and consumer goods, with non-contact versions typically providing safer and more versatile solutions for sensitive environments.</p></p>
