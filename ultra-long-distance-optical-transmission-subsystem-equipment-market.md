<p><p>The Ultra Long Distance Optical Transmission Subsystem Equipment market is divided into two main types: No SBS Suppression and SBS Suppression. </p><p>No SBS Suppression equipment operates without specific measures to prevent Stimulated Brillouin Scattering (SBS), which can limit transmission distance and signal quality. In contrast, SBS Suppression systems incorporate technologies designed to mitigate the effects of SBS, allowing for longer transmission distances and improved signal integrity. This differentiation impacts their applications, performance, and overall market demand.</p></p>
