<p><p>The Ylang Ylang essential oil market comprises three primary types: absolutes, concentrates, and blends. Absolutes are highly concentrated, aromatic extracts obtained through solvent extraction, prized for their rich fragrance. Concentrates are distilled versions that retain essential compounds while offering a more potent aroma than standard oils. Blends combine Ylang Ylang with other essential oils to create unique fragrances and therapeutic profiles, catering to diverse applications in perfumery, aromatherapy, and personal care products, enhancing the overall market appeal.</p></p>
