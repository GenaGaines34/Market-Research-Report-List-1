<p><p>The metal chelates market encompasses three main types: essential nutrients, auxiliary nutrients, and micronutrients. Auxiliary nutrients support metabolic processes but are not directly involved in growth, such as calcium and magnesium. Micronutrients, essential in smaller quantities, include iron, manganese, and zinc, contributing to vital plant functions. Together, these categories enhance soil fertility and improve crop yields, supporting sustainable agricultural practices.</p></p>
