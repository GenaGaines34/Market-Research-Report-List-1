<p><p>The osteosynthesis products market encompasses two primary categories: degradable and nondegradable materials. Degradable materials, such as bioresorbable implants, gradually break down within the body over time, eliminating the need for surgical removal and allowing for natural healing. Nondegradable materials, including metals and certain polymers, provide long-lasting support but require surgical intervention for removal. Both types play crucial roles in orthopedic and trauma surgeries, addressing various patient needs and enhancing recovery processes while ensuring structural integrity during healing.</p></p>
